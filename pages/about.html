The Story

"The Diary," or simply "Suzan" is a web project about a young woman's attempts at writing a diary. The timeline spans from her gap-year after high-school till spring break of senior college. Suzan's goal is to pour her heart out whenever she gets the chance to write, but she often dwells on howmuch she should write and talk about her daily life. Join Suzan's diary-writing journey as she opens up about her life and learns how to express herself with ink.
